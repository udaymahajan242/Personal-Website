  • Basics

  • What is AVEPASS?

    AvePass is a convinient app that makes ordering great food from your favorite local restaurants near your college campus ahead of time.

  • When and where can I use AvePass

    AvePass can be used 24/7 to order your food from our partner resturants..
    We are currently partnered with University Ave resturants near University of Washington (Seattle), coming soon near Seattle University.

  • How much does it cost to use AVEPASS?

    Its FREE to use and order food. Restaurants offer different meals at competitive prices on the AVEPASS app. We do not charge our users any booking charges or memebership fees whatsoever.

  • Does AVEPASS deliver to specified address?

    No, AvePass does not provide this service..

  • From which resturants can I order food?

    We've partnered with top restaurants in Seattle to bring their menus to our customers and are constantly looking to add more.
    Please kindly visit the resturants page for more information.
