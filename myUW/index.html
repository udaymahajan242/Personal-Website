HIGHLIGHTS

Features you will love

Material Design

Beautiful design inspired by Google Material Apps for simple user freindly navigation you are accustomed to on your Android.

Single Sign-on

No need to open your browser and enter url/login details repeatedly.Sign in once, and you are good to go untill you logout or clear app data .

Secure

The app uses the same security as the regular UWNETID sign in so that your information remains visible only to you.
